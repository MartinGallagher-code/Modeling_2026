## Model Overview
The Sharp LH5801 was an 8-bit microprocessor designed by Sharp for use in pocket computers and calculators. It featured a unique architecture optimized for low power consumption and compact code, with no instruction prefetch or pipeline. It was used in the Sharp PC-1500 and PC-1600 pocket computers.
