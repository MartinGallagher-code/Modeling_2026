## Model Overview
The Mostek 3870 was an F8-compatible single-chip microcontroller that integrated the multi-chip F8 design into a single package. It included on-chip RAM, ROM, I/O, and timer, and was faster than the original F8 multi-chip design with a 64-byte scratchpad RAM. It was used in consumer electronics, games, and embedded control applications.
