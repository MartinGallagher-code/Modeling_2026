## Model Overview
The Synertek SY6502A was a licensed second-source for the MOS 6502 microprocessor. It was fully compatible with identical instruction timing. The "A" suffix indicates speed-binned parts rated for 2 MHz operation. Synertek was one of several companies licensed to manufacture the 6502, alongside Rockwell, NCR, and GTE. It was used in various 6502-based systems and Apple II clones.
