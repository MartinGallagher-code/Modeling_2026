# Motorola 68040 - Project Summary

## Overview
Most powerful 68k processor ever (1990). First with on-chip FPU.

## Key Specifications
- **Transistors:** 1,200,000
- **Pipeline:** 6 stages
- **Caches:** 4KB I-cache + 4KB D-cache
- **FPU:** On-chip (first 68k!)
- **Clock:** 25-40 MHz
- **IPC:** ~0.90

## Key Improvements Over 68030
- 4× more transistors
- 16× larger caches
- 2× deeper pipeline
- Integrated FPU (3-5× faster FP)
- 2-3× overall performance

## Famous Systems
- Mac Quadra series (peak 68k Mac era)
- Amiga 4000
- NeXTstation Turbo

## Historical Significance
Peak of 68k architecture. After 68040, Motorola joined PowerPC alliance. Represents limits of CISC complexity.
