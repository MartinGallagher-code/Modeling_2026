# GI PIC1650 - Project Summary

## First PIC Microcontroller (1977)

Originally designed as I/O controller for CP1600, became ancestor of one of most successful MCU families ever.

## Key Features
- Harvard architecture
- 33 simple instructions
- Most execute in 1 cycle
- Extremely cheap

## Why Study It
- Established Harvard MCU architecture
- Pioneered RISC-like simplicity
- Created hobbyist-friendly ecosystem
- Ancestor of billions of PICs
