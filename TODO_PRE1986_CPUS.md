## Selection Criteria

When choosing which to implement next, consider:

1. **Historical significance** - First of its kind, widely influential
2. **Commercial success** - Billions shipped, used in popular products
3. **Architectural interest** - Unique features worth modeling
4. **Data availability** - Datasheets and timing info accessible
5. **Cross-validation potential** - Related to existing models
