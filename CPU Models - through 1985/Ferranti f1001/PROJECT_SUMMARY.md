# Ferranti F100-L - Project Summary

## British Military 16-Bit Processor (1976)

Radiation-hardened processor for UK defense applications.

## Design Priority
1. Reliability
2. Radiation hardness
3. UK supply independence

## Applications
Tornado, Rapier, naval systems, satellites.

## Historical Significance
Represents Cold War era British computing independence. Replaced by commercial rad-hard parts in 1990s.
