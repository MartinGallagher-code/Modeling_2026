# Zilog Z280 - Project Summary

## Failed Z80 Successor (1985)

Ambitious Z80 enhancement with MMU, cache, and 16-bit bus. Commercial failure.

## Features
- 16-bit bus, 16 MB address space
- On-chip MMU with protection
- 256-byte instruction cache
- Hardware multiply/divide

## Why It Failed
- Not fully Z80 compatible
- By 1985, market chose 80286 or 68000
- No software ecosystem

## Lesson
Compatibility matters more than features.
