# RCA CDP1804 - Quick Start

## Enhanced Space Processor (1980)

Middle of 1802 family: 1802 → **1804** → 1805

| Spec | 1802 | 1804 |
|------|------|------|
| On-chip RAM | None | **64B** |
| Timer | None | **Yes** |
| Clock | 2 MHz | **5 MHz** |

## Why Space?
Silicon-on-Sapphire = radiation hardened.

## Family Heritage
- Voyager 1 & 2: 1802 (still running!)
- New Horizons: 1805 (8+ billion km away)
